#1 - Bruh

Overall a good and reliable expression of bruh. Only complaint is that it could use some sort of flair. Due to the short delay, this would be a good bruh button to keep handy in case you have to make everyone laugh after someone does something stupid. Overall 7/10.

#2 - Bruh sound effect #2

As with the first review, this one is quite good and there aren't many complaints. However, the delay between the button press and bruh is quite annoying. If someone did something dumb and you had to play a bruh sound effect on the fly, this might be a problematic one. Overall 5/10.

#3 - Mario Bruh

Funny. Would be cool to play this when someone does something really stupid to make everyone laugh. Overall 9/10.

#4 - Bruhseption

Incblackible. If there was a really huge bruh moment, you could make an action movie-style trailer in iMovie with this in the background. Overall 10/10.

#5 - Ay yo bruh

Eh. I'm not a big fan of the deviation from the original bruh formula. The different voice and the weird inflection makes it original, but not very familiar and wouldn't be a good bruh for a bruh moment. Maybe a good emergency bruh button? Overall 3/10.

#6 - And his name is bruh

Very funny. I can already imagine myself in class playing this after someone drops a pencil. It would make everyone laugh. The original combination of John and Bruh makes this especially good. Overall 9/10.
